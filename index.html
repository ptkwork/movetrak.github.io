About MoveTrak

MoveTrak is a cutting-edge distributed sensing technology designed to monitor movement in care homes, reducing the risk of hospitalizations due to pressure ulcers. Our solution uses state-of-the-art sensors to provide real-time data, ensuring the safety and well-being of residents.
